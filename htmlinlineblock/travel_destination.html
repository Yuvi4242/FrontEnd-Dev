Top 3 Travel Destinations

Destination 1: Paris

Paris is known for art, fashion, and the Eiffel Tower.

“A wonderful city with world-class museums.” — Traveler A

Destination 2: Kyoto

Kyoto offers beautiful temples, gardens, and culture.

“Peaceful and full of history.” — Traveler B

Destination 3: Goa

Goa is famous for beaches, seafood, and vibrant nightlife.

“Relaxing beaches and great local food.” — Traveler C

Travel Tips

  • Check visa requirements in advance.
